Description

Step into the dystopian landscape of Cato's world, an extraordinary stray cat with amazing jump skills, as you embrace the life of a delivery animal in this unforgiving metropolis.



- Navigate through the haunting remnants of what once was a vibrant city
- Get a mission to prove yourself against the treacherous paths around you
- Unleash Cato's unstoppable prowess and get to your destination as fast as no one else
- Advance to your goal while helping others on the way

Discover the hidden truths behind Cato's world downfall and find hope amidst the chaos. Can you lead Cato through the shadows of a broken world, and perhaps, shape a better future?
